About Us

Angelica Santos, Creator

Our mission at MealMachine is to revolutionize the way people approach grocery shopping and meal planning. We aim to provide a seamless, efficient and personalized experience that helps individuals and families to easily formulate and create meals with ingredients that are readily available at home. We at MealMachine's goal is to help reduce waste and cost in each household. Creator of MealMachine, Angelica Santos, is passionate about health and food. While in University, Angelica plans meals week by week trying to balance health, school, relationships and life. Knowing that needing to do grocery shopping and meal plan is arduous, she constructed MealMachine to turn your shopping list into your meal plan companion.
