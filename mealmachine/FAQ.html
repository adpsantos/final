Frequently Asked Questions

Who would typically use MealMachine? -MealMachine is for all age types, young and older. Especially for people who love to create meal plans or find meal planning intimidating. MealMachine intends to make this task easier and helpful for parents who have do not have a lot of time in the day to plan and make meals for their families. People who are into fitness would love MealMachine as well!        Can I share my MealMachine generated recipes to friends and families? -Yes! MealMachine allows you to share your recipes to friends and families. MealMachine tries to make it as integrated as possible between other users. With the "share recipe tab", you would be able to share your creations.        What does the MealMachine logo mean? -The MealMachine logo shows a shopping cart referring to a shopping. Below that is a conveyor belt, referring to the generator part of the app.        Is MealMachine macros friendly? -MealMachine is macros friendly. MealMachine has features that allow you to view your macros and shows you the total nutrition information of the meal.
